India's GDP Growth Slows Down to 5.4%

India's gross domestic product (GDP) growth rate has slowed down to 5.4% in the October-December quarter, according to data released by the government. This is lower than the 6.9% growth recorded in the same quarter last year.

Published on March 22, 2024 by Indian News Agency

State Assembly Elections Scheduled in Andhra Pradesh

The Election Commission of India has announced the schedule for state assembly elections in Andhra Pradesh. The elections are set to take place on April 9, 2024, and the results will be declared on May 2, 2024.
